## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why do I need to allow Health access?
Stepwise Go reads step count data from Apple Health to show your daily progress and activity history.

### Why are step numbers sometimes slightly different between iPhone and Watch?
Live updates and HealthKit aggregation can update at slightly different times. Values usually converge shortly after.

### How do I change my daily step goal?
Open Settings in the app and adjust your daily goal.
