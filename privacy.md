# Privacy Policy for Stepwise Go

_Last updated: April 27, 2026_

Stepwise Go is designed to help you track daily step progress on iPhone and Apple Watch.

## Data We Use

Stepwise Go may access the following data on your device:

- Step count data from Apple Health (HealthKit)
- Motion activity data (Core Motion), while app features that need live updates are active
- App preferences such as daily step goal and language settings

## How Data Is Used

We use this data only to provide app functionality, including:

- Showing your daily step count and progress
- Showing recent step history
- Syncing your daily goal between iPhone and Apple Watch
- Updating watch complications/widgets

## Data Sharing and Selling

- We do not sell your personal data.
- We do not use your health data for advertising.
- We do not share your health data with third parties for marketing.

## Data Storage

- Health data is read from Apple Health on-device.
- Preferences (such as step goal and language) are stored locally on your device.
- If Apple system services (for example iCloud/Health sync managed by Apple) are enabled on your device, Apple may sync data according to Apple’s policies.

## Your Choices

You can control permissions at any time:

- iPhone: Settings -> Privacy & Security -> Health / Motion & Fitness
- Apple Watch: Watch app / system settings for Health permissions

If you deny Health access, parts of Stepwise Go that depend on step data may not function.

## Children’s Privacy

Stepwise Go is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
